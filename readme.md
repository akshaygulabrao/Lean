## Core Feature Set

LEAN is an event-driven, professional-caliber algorithmic trading platform built with a passion for elegant engineering and deep quant concept modeling. Out-of-the-box alternative data and live-trading support.

### Survivorship Bias Free
Automated accounting for splits, dividends, and corporate actions: listings, delistings, mergers, and spins.

### Universe Selection
Avoid selection bias with algorithmically selected assets. Create and select asset universes based on proprietary data and indicators.

### Portfolio Management
Automatically track portfolio performance, profit and loss, buying power, and holdings across multiple asset classes and margin models in the same strategy.

### Scheduled Events
Trigger regular functions to occur at desired times during market hours, on certain days of the week, or at specific times of the day.

### Import Custom Data
Backtest on almost any time series and import your proprietary signal data into your strategy.

## Modular Architecture

LEAN is modular in design, with each component pluggable and customizable. It ships with models for all major plug-in points.

### Slippage and Impact
Use margin, fill, and slippage models to accurately simulate assets and brokerages.

### Technical Indicators
100+ popular technical indicators built, tested, and ready for use. Applicable to any data source.

### Brokerage Models
Simulate brokerage fees, order support, and API behavior nuances with brokerage models.

### Fee Models
Customize fee models to handle rebates and dynamic order pricing.

### Cash Accounts, Settlement, and Margin Models
Simulate T+3 settlement, margin trading, option assignment, and early exercise models.

### Load Proprietary Datasets
Backtest and live trade on your signals, sourced from streaming socket, database, or file sources.
